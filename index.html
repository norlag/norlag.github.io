About

From silicon to software

I'm a Senior Embedded Firmware Engineer with deep expertise in ARM Cortex-M microcontrollers, primarily STM32 family. I design and implement firmware that's efficient, reliable, and production-ready.

My approach emphasizes minimal ISRs, state-machine-driven architectures, and clean separation between hardware abstraction and application logic. I work across the full stack — from reading datasheets and writing register-level drivers to integrating FreeRTOS and debugging hardware through JTAG/SWD.

Based in Lausanne, Switzerland, I believe great firmware is invisible: it just works, every time, under every condition.
